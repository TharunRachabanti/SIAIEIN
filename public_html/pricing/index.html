FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on your specific business needs, workflow complexity, expected ROI, the number of isolated agents needed, and your required level of support.

Is there a setup fee?

Yes, there is a one-time implementation fee based on complexity. This covers building the custom logic, training your team, and a 30-day onboarding period.

Do you offer a guarantee?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on the implementation fee if you do not see the agreed-upon value.

What if my needs change?

Our custom pricing model is flexible. You can easily add more agents or scale down capabilities as your business evolves.

How do I get started?

Simply schedule a free consultation call. We will discuss your workflows and build a custom proposal for you.
